rec-cons(clojure.contrib.seq-utils)

Macro

  • (rec-cons binding-form expr & rec-exprs)

Similar to lazy-cons but binds the resulting sequence using the supplied binding-form, allowing recursive expressions. The first expression must not be recursive.

; seq_utils.clj:103
(defmacro rec-cons 
 [binding-form expr & rec-exprs]
  `(let [rec-rest# (atom nil)
         result# (lazy-cons ~expr (force @rec-rest#))
         ~binding-form result#]
     (swap! rec-rest# (constantly (delay (lazy-cat ~@rec-exprs))))
     result#))
