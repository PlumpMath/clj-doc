take-while(clojure.core)

  • (take-while pred coll)

Returns a lazy seq of successive items from coll while (pred item) returns true. pred must be free of side-effects.

; core.clj:1471
(defn take-while
  [pred coll]
    (when (and (seq coll) (pred (first coll)))
      (lazy-cons (first coll) (take-while pred (rest coll)))))
