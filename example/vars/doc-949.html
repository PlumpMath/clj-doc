rec-cat(clojure.contrib.seq-utils)

Macro

  • (rec-cat binding-name & exprs)

Similar to lazy-cat but binds the resulting sequence to the supplied binding-name, allowing for recursive expressions.

; clojure/contrib/seq_utils.clj:104
(defmacro rec-cat 
 [binding-name & exprs]
  `(rec-seq ~binding-name (lazy-cat ~@exprs)))
