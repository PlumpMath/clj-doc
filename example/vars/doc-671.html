deliver(clojure.core)

  • (deliver promise val)

Experimental. Delivers the supplied value to the promise, releasing any pending derefs. A subsequent call to deliver on a promise will throw an exception.

; clojure/core.clj:4387
(defn deliver
  [promise val] (promise val))
