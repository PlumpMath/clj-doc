rec-seq(clojure.contrib.seq-utils)

Macro

  • (rec-seq binding-name & body)

Similar to lazy-seq but binds the resulting seq to the supplied binding-name, allowing for recursive expressions.

; clojure/contrib/seq_utils.clj:97
(defmacro rec-seq 
 [binding-name & body]
  `(let [s# (atom nil)]
     (reset! s# (lazy-seq (let [~binding-name @s#] ~@body)))))
