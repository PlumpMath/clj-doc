append-child(clojure.zip)

  • (append-child loc item)

Inserts the item as the rightmost child of the node at this loc, without moving

; clojure/zip.clj:194
(defn append-child
  [loc item]
    (replace loc (make-node loc (node loc) (concat (children loc) [item]))))
