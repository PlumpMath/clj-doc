apply(clojure.core)

  • (apply f args* argseq)

Applies fn f to the argument list formed by prepending args to argseq.

; core.clj:404
(defn apply
  {:arglists '([f args* argseq])}
  [#^clojure.lang.IFn f & args]
    (. f (applyTo (spread args))))
