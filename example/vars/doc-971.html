repeatedly(clojure.core)

  • (repeatedly f)

Takes a function of no args, presumably with side effects, and returns an infinite lazy sequence of calls to it

; clojure/core.clj:3307
(defn repeatedly
  [f] (lazy-seq (cons (f) (repeatedly f))))
