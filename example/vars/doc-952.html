reductions(clojure.contrib.seq-utils)

  • (reductions f coll)
  • (reductions f init coll)

Returns a lazy seq of the intermediate values of the reduction (as per reduce) of coll by f, starting with init.

; clojure/contrib/seq_utils.clj:113
(defn reductions
  ([f coll]
   (if (seq coll)
     (rec-seq self (cons (first coll) (map f self (rest coll))))
     (cons (f) nil)))
  ([f init coll]
   (rec-seq self (cons init (map f self coll)))))
