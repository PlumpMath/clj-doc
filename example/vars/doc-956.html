ref-min-history(clojure.core)

Gets the min-history of a ref, or sets it and returns the ref

; clojure/core.clj:1456
(defn ref-min-history
  ([#^clojure.lang.Ref ref]
    (.getMinHistory ref))
  ([#^clojure.lang.Ref ref n]
    (.setMinHistory ref n)))
