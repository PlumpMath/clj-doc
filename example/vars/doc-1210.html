  • (into to from)

Returns a new coll consisting of to-coll with all of the items of from-coll conjoined.

; core.clj:1872
(defn into
  [to from]
    (let [ret to items (seq from)]
      (if items
        (recur (conj ret (first items)) (next items))
        ret)))
