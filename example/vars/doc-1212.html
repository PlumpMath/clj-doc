lazy-cat(clojure.core)

Macro

  • (lazy-cat coll)
  • (lazy-cat coll & colls)

Expands to code which yields a lazy sequence of the concatenation of the supplied colls. Each coll expr is not evaluated until it is needed.

; core.clj:2577
(defmacro lazy-cat
  ([coll] `(seq ~coll))
  ([coll & colls]
   `(let [iter# (fn iter# [coll#]
		    (if (seq coll#)
		      (lazy-cons (first coll#) (iter# (rest coll#)))
		      (lazy-cat ~@colls)))]
      (iter# ~coll))))
