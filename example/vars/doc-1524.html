write-lines(clojure.contrib.duck-streams)

  • (write-lines f lines)

Writes lines (a seq) to f, separated by newlines. f is opened with writer.

; duck_streams.clj:159
(defn write-lines
  [f lines]
  (with-open [#^PrintWriter writer (writer f)]
    (loop [lines lines]
      (when-let [line (first lines)]
        (.write writer (str line))
        (.println writer)
        (recur (rest lines))))))
