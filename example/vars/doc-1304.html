partition-all(clojure.contrib.seq-utils)

  • (partition-all n coll)
  • (partition-all n step coll)

Returns a lazy sequence of lists like clojure.core/partition, but may include lists with fewer than n items at the end.

; seq_utils.clj:129
(defn partition-all
  ([n coll]
     (partition-all n n coll))
  ([n step coll]
     (lazy-seq
      (when-let [s (seq coll)]
        (cons (take n s) (partition-all n step (drop step s)))))))
